Company Overview

Specialist research, advisory and corporate training solutions across Africa.

ACORSER supports businesses, institutions and public sector organisations through research, regulatory advisory, compliance systems, project support, intellectual property management, strategic management services, marketing research, taxation, and debt recovery solutions.

Regional presence South Africa headquarters with representative interests in Ghana, Nigeria, Cameroon, Rwanda, Kenya, Tanzania, Namibia, Zimbabwe, and Botswana.
Growth story First formed in 2005, expanded its scope in 2008, and grew from four clients to more than fifty consistent clients.
Vision To lead in core markets of fast-moving corporate services through superior service delivery and strong stakeholder partnerships.
Mission To be a world-class firm where research and services are outstanding, tailored, and delivered for the success of clients.
